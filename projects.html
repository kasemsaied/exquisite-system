Typical Project Information We Work With

To prepare a clear technical and commercial offer, we usually request a basic set of information from consultants, contractors or owners.

Required from the client (as available)

  • Architectural drawings showing window and door locations
  • Elevations and sections for aluminum and façade works
  • BOQ or schedule of openings (if already prepared)
  • Any existing specifications for aluminum, glass or blinds
  • Expected timeline for award and execution on site
What we provide in return
  • Technical review and clarification of aluminum, blind and shutter scopes
  • Proposed systems and materials matching project level
  • Commercial offer based on received quantities and drawings
  • Optional shop drawings after award for consultant approval
