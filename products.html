Products & Technical Solutions

Exquisite System Industry Company provides aluminum systems, interior shading and shutter solutions that can be combined as a complete package or supplied separately, depending on project scope and budget.

Aluminum Windows, Doors & Façades

Our aluminum fabrication covers typical building envelopes: windows, doors, shopfronts and curtain wall façades. We work with profiles and glass types suited to the building category and local climate.

Residential buildings Commercial centers Office towers Industrial buildings (doors & louvers)
Windows & doors
Sliding & Hinged Systems

For villas, apartments, offices and small shops.

  • Sliding windows and balcony doors
  • Hinged doors for main and side entrances
  • Fixed glass panels & showcases
  • Available with single or double glazing
Façades
Curtain Wall & Shopfronts

Glass façades for modern commercial buildings.

  • Aluminum curtain wall systems
  • Frameless and framed shopfronts
  • Integration with sliding & hinged doors
  • Coordination of glass thickness and tint
Special items
Louvers & Utility Openings

For mechanical rooms and service areas.

  • Aluminum louvers for ventilation openings
  • Service doors for technical rooms
  • Protective grills where required
Typical specifications can be aligned with the project consultant’s requirements. Upon receiving the project drawings and BOQ, we can match or propose alternative aluminum profiles, glass types and accessories to suit the budget and performance targets.

Interior Shading & Roller Blinds

We supply and install interior shading solutions ranging from simple blackout blinds for bedrooms to complete shading packages for office floors and hospitality projects.

Offices & meeting rooms Hotels & furnished apartments Hospitals & clinics Residential units
Roller blinds
Blackout & Sunscreen Blinds

For precise light control and privacy.

  • Blackout fabrics for bedrooms and meeting rooms
  • Sunscreen fabrics for offices and living areas
  • Manual chain-operated or motorized systems
  • Single or double-layer blinds where required
Curtain solutions
Curtains for Hotels & Residences

Decorative and functional curtain systems.

  • Sheer and blackout curtain combinations
  • Tracks and rails suitable for heavy fabrics
  • Hotel-style window treatments
  • Design coordination with interior designer
Controls
Motors & Control Options

Comfort and convenience for modern buildings.

  • Tubular motors for roller blinds
  • Wall switches and remote controls
  • Group control for multiple blinds
  • Integration-ready for building control systems (where applicable)
For larger projects, we can provide a schedule of blinds and curtains by room or area, indicating fabric type, control method and color selection, to be coordinated with the interior design package.

External Shutter Systems

Our shutter solutions provide security, privacy and light control for villas, shops and industrial openings. We support both manual and motorized options.

Villas & private homes Retail shops & showrooms Warehouses & loading bays
Residential shutters
Villas & Apartments

For bedrooms, living rooms and balconies.

  • Insulated aluminum slats for noise and heat reduction
  • Manual or motorized operation
  • Different colors to match façade design
  • Optional perforation for partial light
Commercial shutters
Shops & Showrooms

Security with convenient daily operation.

  • Heavy-duty side rails and bottom profiles
  • Perforated slats for visibility when required
  • Motorized operation with key switches
  • Emergency manual override (as applicable)
Industrial shutters
Warehouses & Service Doors

For larger openings and frequent use.

  • Shutters sized for loading bays and service entries
  • Motors sized for frequent operation
  • Optional safety edges and photocells (where specified)
  • Coordination with steel structure and opening details
When sending an inquiry for shutters, please provide approximate opening widths and heights, preferred operation method (manual or motorized) and any specific security or visibility requirements, so we can propose a suitable solution.

Turn-key Supply & Installation Packages

In many projects, aluminum, blinds and shutters are combined into one package to simplify coordination and avoid gaps between different suppliers.

Residential Building Package

Aluminum windows and doors, roller blinds for bedrooms and living areas, and shutters for selected openings in villas or residential buildings.

  • Suitable for small compounds and villa projects
  • One package, one point of contact
  • Better coordination of colors and finishes
Office & Commercial Package

Aluminum shopfronts and façades, interior blinds for office floors and meeting rooms, with optional shutters for back-of-house areas.

  • Aligned with consultant drawings
  • Roller blinds coordinated with ceiling and lighting
  • Flexible phasing to match project schedule
Industrial Package

Shutters for loading bays and service doors, aluminum for office parts in industrial buildings, and basic shading for administration areas.

  • Focus on durability and easy operation
  • Integration with steel structures
  • Option to add safety accessories as specified
