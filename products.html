Products & Solutions

Exquisite System Industry Co. offers a complete range of aluminum and shading solutions for residential, commercial and industrial buildings. Below is an overview of our main product groups.

Aluminum Systems

Windows & Doors
Residential & Commercial Profiles

Sliding and hinged systems suitable for villas, apartments, showrooms and offices.

  • Single and double-glazed options
  • Thermal and non-thermal systems
  • Standard and custom colors (powder coating)
Curtain Walls & Façades
Modern Building Envelopes

Aluminum façade systems for commercial buildings, malls and towers.

  • Stick and unitized curtain wall systems
  • Spider and structural glazing solutions
  • Sunshade and cladding integration
Special Aluminum Works
Handrails, Skylights & Others

Custom aluminum works designed according to project needs and site conditions.

  • Handrails and balustrades
  • Skylights and roof lights
  • Louvers and ventilation grills

Curtains, Roller Blinds & Shading

Roller Blinds
Manual & Motorized Systems

Practical shading solutions for offices, schools, healthcare and residential projects.

  • Blackout, dim-out and sunscreen fabrics
  • Chain-operated and motorized options
  • Integration with smart home systems
Curtains
Interior Decorative Curtains

Fabric curtains for living rooms, bedrooms, hotel rooms and hospitality facilities.

  • Wide fabric selections and colors
  • Sheer and blackout combinations
  • Tracks, rails and decorative accessories
Outdoor Shading
Pergolas & External Shades

Shading solutions to enhance outdoor comfort and reduce heat load on buildings.

  • Aluminum pergolas and canopies
  • Fabric tensile shades (in cooperation with partners)
  • Custom sizes and anchoring details

Shutter Systems & Security Solutions

Residential Shutters
Villas & Apartments

Shutters for windows and doors to provide privacy, light control and additional security.

  • Insulated aluminum profiles
  • Manual and motorized operation
  • Color customization to match façade
Commercial & Industrial Shutters
Shops, Warehouses & Loading Bays

Heavy-duty shutters for commercial and industrial facilities.

  • Solid, perforated or grille-type designs
  • Remote control and safety sensors
  • High-cycle motors for intensive use
Turn-key Packages
Supply, Installation & Maintenance

Complete shutter and aluminum packages, including fabrication, delivery and installation on site.

  • Site survey and measurements
  • Technical recommendations
  • After-sales support and spare parts
