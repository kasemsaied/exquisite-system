About Exquisite System Industry Company

Exquisite System Industry Co. is a Saudi company specialized in aluminum works, shading solutions, curtains, roller blinds and shutter systems for residential, commercial and industrial buildings across the Kingdom.

Who We Are

We operate as an integrated partner for contractors, consultants and building owners. Our scope covers design, shop drawings, material selection, fabrication, delivery and on-site installation of aluminum and shading systems.

With licensed industrial activities, VAT registration and branches in Riyadh, Jeddah and Dammam, we are able to support both small projects and large-scale developments across Saudi Arabia.

  • Saudi-based industrial company
  • Dedicated aluminum fabrication and assembly
  • Specialized in shading, roller blinds and shutters
  • Responsive after-sales and maintenance support

Key Facts

  • Branches in Riyadh, Jeddah and Dammam
  • Serving residential, commercial and industrial clients
  • Turn-key supply and installation services
  • Flexible solutions tailored to project needs and budget

Our Vision

To be a trusted Saudi reference in aluminum and shading systems, delivering durable, modern and high-performance solutions for buildings nationwide.

Mission & Core Values

Our mission is to provide reliable aluminum and shading solutions that combine engineering quality, aesthetic design and practical performance, while ensuring on-time execution and excellent customer service.

Quality

Commitment

Service

Development
