About Exquisite System Industry Company

A Saudi industrial company specialized in aluminum works, curtains, roller blinds and shutter systems, serving residential, commercial and industrial projects across the Kingdom.

Who we are

Exquisite System Industry Company operates with a clear commitment to quality, reliability and on-time delivery. We combine technical expertise with practical field experience to support contractors, consultants and building owners in achieving their project goals.

From our branches in Riyadh, Jeddah and Dammam, we manage projects of different sizes – villas, residential buildings, commercial centers, offices, hotels and industrial facilities.

Mission
To deliver high-quality aluminum and shading solutions that meet project specifications and add long-term value to our clients’ assets.
Vision
To be a trusted partner for aluminum, roller blinds and shutter systems in the Saudi market.
Values
Integrity, professionalism, safety, commitment, continuous improvement and customer focus.
Team
Engineers, technicians and installers with hands-on experience in local and regional projects.
Experience
Our team brings years of experience in aluminum profiles, façade systems, interior shading and shutter technologies, ensuring solutions that are both aesthetic and functional.
Quality & Compliance
We follow project specifications and relevant standards, select reliable materials and apply workmanship procedures that enhance durability and performance.
Partnership Approach
We work as partners with consultants and contractors – providing technical proposals, samples, shop drawings and on-site coordination to keep the project on schedule.
