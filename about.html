About Exquisite System Industry Company

Exquisite System Industry Co. is a Saudi industrial company specialized in aluminum works, glass façades, interior shading and shutter systems. We support projects from early design and shop drawings to fabrication, delivery and on-site installation.

Our role is to connect project specifications with practical execution. We review drawings and requirements with consultants and contractors, select suitable aluminum systems and materials, and then manufacture and install according to approved details and timelines.

From residential villas and apartment buildings to commercial centers, offices and industrial facilities, we provide solutions that balance aesthetics, performance and cost-efficiency.

What we focus on

Clear coordination, reliable delivery and consistent technical quality. We believe that aluminum and shading works should not be a bottleneck in the project schedule, but a package that is planned, executed and handed over smoothly.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a trusted industrial partner in aluminum and shading systems for building projects across Saudi Arabia, recognized for reliability, technical clarity and professional execution.

Our Mission

To provide practical, durable and well-engineered aluminum, roller blind and shutter solutions that help consultants, contractors and owners deliver successful buildings on time and within budget.

How we work with your project

Our typical workflow ensures that aluminum and shading works follow a clear and predictable path:

1
Initial inquiry

We receive drawings, BOQ or a simple description of the required aluminum, blinds or shutters.

2
Site visit & measurements

When needed, we visit the site to confirm dimensions, conditions and installation constraints.

3
Proposal & shop drawings

We prepare a technical and commercial offer, and issue shop drawings for review and approval.

4
Fabrication & installation

After approval, we proceed with fabrication, delivery and installation according to the agreed schedule.

5
Testing & handover

Systems are checked, adjusted and handed over, with guidance on operation and basic maintenance.
