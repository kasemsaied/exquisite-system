About Exquisite System Industry Co.

Exquisite System Industry Company is a Saudi industrial company specialized in aluminum works, glass façades and complete shading solutions. We support our clients from design and shop drawings to fabrication, delivery and installation on site.

End-to-end service
Design, Fabrication & Installation

We coordinate with consultants and contractors on profiles, glass types and technical details, then manufacture and install according to project specifications and timelines.

Industrial presence
Riyadh · Jeddah · Dammam

Our branches and teams enable us to handle projects in different regions of the Kingdom, from small buildings to large commercial and industrial developments.

Quality & reliability
Materials & After-sales

We rely on proven aluminum systems, fabric suppliers and motor brands, and provide after-sales support to keep shutters and shading systems working smoothly.
