Engineering and project delivery approach

For consultants and contractors, clarity of scope and documentation is as important as product quality. Our process is structured to support engineering and site teams from tender to hand-over.

Engineering support

  • Review of architectural drawings and BOQ items.
  • Suggestions for alternative systems where appropriate.
  • Preparation of aluminum and shading shop drawings.
  • Submittal of samples and technical data for approval.
  • Coordination with MEP and civil where openings are critical.
Clear technical and commercial breakdown:

Offers can separate aluminum, glass, shading and shutters into distinct line items or combine them into a single package, depending on how the consultant and contractor prefer to manage the contract. This makes it easier to evaluate options and maintain cost control.

01
Tender & technical proposal

Review project documents, clarify scope and submit a technical and commercial proposal, with alternatives where beneficial.

02
Shop drawings & approvals

Prepare shop drawings, samples and data sheets for aluminum, shading and shutters until final consultant approval.

03
Fabrication & phased delivery

Fabricate systems based on approved drawings, with phased delivery aligned with site readiness and project schedule.

04
Installation & hand-over

Installation by trained teams, snag list closure, and hand-over of the completed scope with guidance on operation and maintenance.
